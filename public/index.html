About Us

We are Roy, Angela, Nathan and Luke Verrips, a Christian family living in Napervill, Illinois. We are originally from South Africa, but spend almost 15 years living in the Middle East.


We moved from Dubai towards then end of 2016, where we lived for most of our time in the gulf, but in between a few moves within Dubai, we have also lived a year in Abu Dhabi, and two years in Doha, Qatar. We are a bible believing Christian family who strive to put God at the center of our household.


We are currently still deciding on a new church home here in Naperville. We pray and trust that God will continue to strengthen us in His mercy and grace, and that we may be a blessing to those He brings across our path.


Our God is a gracious and merciful God!


Yours in Him
