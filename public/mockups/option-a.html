About Us

We are Roy, Angela, Nathan and Luke Verrips, a Christian family living in Reidville, South Carolina since the summer of 2022 — which is also when Angela's father, Don Kirkwood, came to live with us.

Originally from South Africa, before immigrating to the US in 2016 we spent about 15 years living in the Middle East — Dubai, Abu Dhabi, and Doha, Qatar.

We pray and trust that God will continue to strengthen us in His mercy and grace, and that we may be a blessing to those He brings across our path.

"Our Saviour is Gentle and Lowly in heart." — Matthew 11:29

Members of Reidville Presbyterian Church in America
