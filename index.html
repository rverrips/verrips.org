About Us

We are Roy, Angela, Nathan and Luke Verrips, a Christian family living in Doha, Qatar.. We are originally from South Africa, but have been living in the Middle East for over a decade.


We lived in Dubai for about nine years, then after a year in Abu Dhabi, are now settled in Doha since late 2012. We are a bible believing Christian family who strive to put God at the center of our household.


The church we are part of here in Doha is called Grace Fellowship. We pray and trust that God will continue to strengthen us in His mercy and grace, and that we may be a blessing to those He brings across our path.


Our God is a gracious and merciful God!


Yours in Him
